Get residents’ or visitors’ parking permits

Get permits for some areas. Other areas will be available online soon.

You'll need:

  • an email address to create your account
  • a debit or credit card to pay (1.8% extra charge for credit cards)
  • vehicle registration certificate V5C (log book) for each vehicle - not needed if you want only visitors' permits

You must answer all questions unless marked as optional.
